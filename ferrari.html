Ferrari 488 Pista

The Ferrari 488 Pista is a track-focused version of the Ferrari 488 GTB, featuring a 3.9-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine producing a mind-blowing 711 horsepower and 568 lb-ft of torque. It's engineered to provide unmatched performance and driving dynamics, especially on the track.

Key Specifications

Engine

3.9L Twin-Turbo V8

Power

711 hp @ 8,000 rpm

Torque

568 lb-ft @ 3,000 rpm

0-60 mph

2.85 seconds

Top Speed

211 mph (340 km/h)

Transmission

7-speed dual-clutch transmission
